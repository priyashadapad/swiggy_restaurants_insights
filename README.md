# Swiggy Restaurants Data Analysis 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes restaurant data from Swiggy to understand food trends, pricing, ratings, delivery time, and restaurant distribution across cities and areas in India.  
The dashboard is built using **Power BI** to provide interactive and visual insights.

---

## Key Metrics
- **Average Rating:** 3.66  
- **Average Price:** ₹348.44  
- **Total Restaurants:** 8,680  
- **Average Delivery Time:** 53.97 minutes  

---

## Dashboard Insights
- **Top 5 Most Served Food Types**  
  Identifies the most popular food categories based on restaurant count.
  
- **Top 10 Areas with Most Restaurants**  
  Shows areas having the highest number of restaurants.

- **Top 10 Cities with Most Restaurants (Map View)**  
  Visual representation of restaurant concentration across Indian cities.

- **Most Expensive Areas (Average Price-wise)**  
  Highlights areas where the average food price is highest.

- **Filters**  
  - City  
  - Area  
  (Allows interactive analysis)
